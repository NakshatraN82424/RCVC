About

This website was created by a student to support the organization and management of her American Red Cross Volunteer club. It serves as a platform where members are able to log and track their volunteer hours, view their service history, and stay informed about club activities and announcements. The site was deisgned to simplify record-keeping and improve club communication. By providing an efficient and user-friendly system for documenting service, this website helps clubs stay organized and focused on making a meaningful impact in the community.
